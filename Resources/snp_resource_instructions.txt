This document describes the steps taken to acquire the historical data for the S&P500 Index.

1. Navigate to https://nasdaq.com/market-activity/index/spx/historical
2. Select "5Y" above the chart
3. Click "Download Data" to save to a csv file

Notes:
1. Result returns 1259 rows
2. Last row dated 6/1/2016 with a close price of $2099.33
